The Five Strands

Choose a strand to open its lessons and textbook.

Number

Reading and writing numbers to 9999; comparing, ordering and rounding; fractions; and adding, subtracting, multiplying and dividing.

Place value · Fractions · Operations Open strand

Measurement

Measuring with the right units; length, mass and capacity; telling time; counting money and giving change; and finding area and perimeter.

Length · Time · Money · Area Open strand

Geometry

Flat (2D) shapes and solid (3D) shapes and their parts; making shape patterns; lines of symmetry; and area as covering a surface.

Shapes · Patterns · Symmetry Open strand

Algebra

Finding and continuing number patterns; using a box or letter for an unknown; finding the missing number; true-or-false sentences; and “if–then” puzzles.

Patterns · Unknowns · Sentences Open strand

Statistics & Probability

Collecting and sorting information with tallies and tables; making and reading pictographs and bar graphs; and using chance words like certain and likely.

Data · Graphs · Probability Open strand
